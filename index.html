THE DPU GREEN HERITAGE PROJECT
 is presented by students of computer department of our college.It is a dedicated initiative aimed at preserving and promoting the biodiversity of the Dr. D. Y. Patil ACS College. This website serves as an interactive platform to showcase the vibrant flora that thrives within the college grounds. From the towering Neem Tree (Azadirachta indica) to the Mango tree(Magnifera Indica). The site highlights the importance of each plant in fostering sustainability and environmental awareness. With an engaging and visually appealing design, the website aims to inspire visitors to appreciate the natural heritage around them and encourages participation in eco-friendly practices. After clicking the image of plant which is given below, you will get summarize information as well as an unique feature of that plant.
